FAQs

What is AgroBox NG? + -

AgroBox NG is a subscription service that delivers boxes of fresh, locally sourced, and sustainable produce directly to your door. We partner with local farmers to bring you the best of the season's harvest.

Where do you source your produce? + -

We work with a network of trusted local farmers across Nigeria who are committed to sustainable and ethical farming practices. We prioritize seasonal produce to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Is your produce organic? + -

While not all our farmers are certified organic, we prioritize farmers who use sustainable and natural farming methods, minimizing or eliminating the use of harmful chemicals. We are transparent about our sourcing and can provide more specific information about individual farms.

Can I customize my box? + -

While we curate our boxes to provide a balanced selection of seasonal produce, we understand you may have preferences. Currently, we do not offer full customization. However, you can let us know if there are items you don't want, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request, depending on availability.
